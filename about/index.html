Fix'r Up

Your local redneck mechanic!

Not Goin Nowhere


Our great great grand-pappy started this business long time ago. He helped the locals get what needed fixin', fixed. And fixed PDQ. Since we are third generation mechanics, we learnt everything we could need to know about cars from our dad. No instruction books needed. The state said we had to get some licenses and insurance and stuff so we went and did that too. But they don't come round here much which gives us the chance to make custom jobs a hit with our customers. And we do the state inspections too so they are guaranteed to pass. We promise!


Every year we host the chili cook-off free of charge to help feed the locals, we get the flea market goin and everyone has a blast. Donations are accepted at the shop. During our free time we like to hunt and fish. Everyone is invited to come and join!


Repair work guaranteed or its free!*

*All work is accepted as is at time of payment and no warranty shall be extented once the vehicle has left the premises.
