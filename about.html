Fix'r Up

Your local redneck mechanic!

Billy

My great great grand-pappy started this business long time ago. He helped the locals get what needed fixin', fixed. And fixed PDQ. Since I'm a third generation mechanic, I learnt everything I could need to know about cars from my dad. Since the recession, we had to get creative with how we work, but that is what makes our custom jobs a hit with our customers with state inspections that are guaranteed to pass.


I supervise two apprentices in troubleshooting, diagnosing, and repairing a wide variety of vehicle makes and models. I also manage day-to-day schedule and operations of the repair shop, maintaining strong focus on quality workmanship and customer service.

Jean

Every year we host the chili cook-off free of charge to help feed the locals, we get the flea market goin and everyone has a blast. Donations are accepted at the shop. During our free time we like to hunt and fish. Everyone is invited to come and join! This picture is of me and my wife, pregnant with our 9th kid, she works double shifts at the Tumbleweed Diner, and makes the best apple pie!


I am a highly skilled and dependable mechanic with hands-on experience conducting service, diagnostics, and repairs on domestic and imported automobiles and trucks. I demonstrated expertise in interpreting technical specifications. I try to stay caught up with current technologies, but we don't get too many newer cars round here.
